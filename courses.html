Welcome Announcement to Math 1101

Welcome to College Algebra! I am looking forward to being your instructor for the upcoming term. Many of you have asked about extra credit opportunities in this class. I wanted to give you a couple options that are related to the presentation aspect of the course.

1. Speakers’ Showcase

Each semester I have special opportunity to choose one of you to represent our class at the Speakers’ Showcase. All communications professors can nominate one student to submit their best speech. If you are chosen to be in the Showcase, you will receive additional extra credit and possibly money or prizes. This is a win-win! The next showcase is April 26th.
